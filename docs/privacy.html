Attic Privacy Policy

Antique Identifier & Value

Effective July 7, 2026

The short version

Attic has no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs. We collect as little as technically possible.

What leaves your device

When you scan an item, the photo you take is sent over an encrypted connection to our image-analysis provider to identify the item and estimate its value. The photo is used only to produce that answer and is not used to train models by us. We do not attach your name, email, or any account identifier to it, because Attic has no accounts.

Your collection (photos, names, notes, rooms, values) is stored only on your device. We cannot see it. Deleting the app deletes it.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Apple provides us anonymous receipts to unlock Pro features.

Children

Attic is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
