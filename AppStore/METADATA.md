## Description
Point your camera at anything old and find out what it is and what it's really worth.

That lamp from your grandmother's house. The strange brass thing at the estate sale. The painting in the thrift store bin. Attic identifies it, estimates what it actually sells for, and files it into a beautiful home inventory.

WHAT IT DOES
- Identify almost anything old: furniture, ceramics, silver, art, tools, toys, glassware, clocks and more
- Honest sold-price ranges, not wishful asking prices
- One tap to real eBay sold listings for your exact item
- The Attic: your whole collection, organized by room and box, with a running estimated total
- "Worth a second look" flags for potential high-value finds
- Notes, rooms and boxes so you never lose track of what's where

THE MOMENT
You know the feeling: you're holding something old and you have no idea whether it's $5 junk or a $2,000 treasure. Attic answers in seconds.

ATTIC PRO
3 identifications are free. Attic Pro is $4.99/month or $29.99/year, auto-renews until cancelled, cancel anytime in Settings.

Estimates are informational, not formal appraisals. For insurance or sale of high-value items, consult a certified appraiser.

## Review notes
Scan flow requires the camera or a library photo. Estimates are clearly labeled "an estimate, not an appraisal" in-app. Paywall appears only after the first full result. No account required, no data collected beyond the photo sent for analysis at scan time.
